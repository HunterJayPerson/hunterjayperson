Hunter Jay

I’m an engineer and entrepreneur trying to make sure AI is developed without killing everybody. I was born in 1996 and grew up in western Sydney, Australia. My Dad is a plasterer, and my Mum a bookkeeper.

I am atheist, vegan, disappointingly AMAB, tall and lanky, and have ‘traces of aspergers’ according to my childhood school. Politically I lean libertarian, but no label really fits. My favourite media includes Feynman’s autobiographies, HPMOR, Legally Blonde the Musical, and Magic Sword. I’ve worked mainly as a CEO and software & robotics engineer, but have also had various gigs as an fast food cook, actor, science tutor, children’s entertainer, video editor, and drag queen.

In 2016, I was studying Physics & Mathematics at USyd when I realised that superintelligence would become the most important thing for humanity to get right in my lifetime. I switched my major to Computer Science, and began working on generating resources and knowledge that could be useful for aligning superintelligence, on the margin. I’ve been doing that ever since.
